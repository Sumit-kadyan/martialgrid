# Tourney Hub (formerly Martial Grid)

> The ultimate multi-sport tournament management ecosystem. Launch events, manage squads, and broadcast live telemetry to thousands of fans.

## About Us
Tourney Hub is a next-generation sports management platform built for organizers, coaches, and fans. We replace messy spreadsheets and chaotic WhatsApp groups with automated bracket engines, persistent team registries, and real-time live scoring.

## Core Features
* **Auto-Bracket Engine:** Instantly generate rounds, matchups, and byes for any tournament size.
* **Live Pulse Telemetry:** Real-time, zero-latency match scoring and event broadcasting.
* **The Squad Registry:** A persistent database where coaches manage team identities and player rosters.
* **Omni-Sport Support:** Dynamic rules engines supporting Cricket, Football, Badminton, Wrestling, Kabaddi, Tennis, Basketball, and Volleyball.
